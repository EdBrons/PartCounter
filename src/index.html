Part Counter v1

Instructions

Step 1: Upload an Image

Take a photo of a tray of parts, like the one on the right.

Click browse and select the image from your computer.

Click upload to scan the image for parts.

Step 2: Usage

The program scans the image for parts, if it finds a part it covers it with a blue rectangle.

The program does not always discover all the parts. You will have to manually add the parts it does not find.

Click and drag the mouse to move the camera.

Press P to start placing a part. Then click to start placing a rectangle, move you mouse to the opposite corner of the part and click again.
